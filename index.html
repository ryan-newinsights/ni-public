The Process

Three steps from repository to documentation.

1

Analyse

NewInsights parses your codebase using a production-grade syntax parser to build a precise dependency map. Every class, function, and module relationship is resolved before any AI processing begins.

2

Generate

A coordinated team of AI agents works through your codebase in dependency order. The Reader understands context. The Searcher finds relevant references. The Writer produces documentation to your standards.

3

Verify

A dedicated Verifier agent reviews every output against quality criteria before it is accepted. Outputs that don't meet the standard are rejected and rewritten — automatically.
